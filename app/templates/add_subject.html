Add a subject

Follow the steps below to find your course calendar link from the IITM BS student dashboard.

1
Open the IITM BS student dashboard
Go to study.iitm.ac.in and sign in with your student credentials.
2
Open the subject
Click on the course you want to track — DSA, DBMS, Maths, or any active subject.
3
Go to the Important Links tab
Inside the subject page, switch to the Important Links tab in the top navigation.
4
Copy the calendar link
Find the Google Calendar link listed on that page. Right-click → Copy link, or click to open it and copy the URL from your browser.
5
Paste it in the form →
If the subject's calendar link is already stored, it will auto-fill. Otherwise, paste the link manually.
